Our Story

From Garbage to Global

It started with a simple idea in 2020: what if trading locally was as easy as sending a text? We built DealSquare to bridge the gap between community trust and modern technology. Today, we're a team of dreamers and doers, redefining how the world buys, sells, and connects.
